Scholarship Opportunity with NSBE

Scholarship Name: NSBE Excellence Scholarship

Deadline: April 30, 2023

Eligibility: The NSBE Excellence Scholarship is open to undergraduate and graduate students pursuing degrees in STEM fields. Applicants must...

  • Be NSBE members in good standing
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Demonstrate leadership and involvement in community or NSBE activities

Application Materials:

  • Completed application form
  • Transcript of academic records
  • Two letters of recommendation

How to Apply: Interested candidates should submit their applications by email to scholarships@nsbe.org by the specified deadline.
